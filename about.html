About Me

I am a second-year student at the University of Texas at Austin studying Computer Science. I built this website to help myself visualize what I have been learning in my Algorithms and Complexity class. Turning abstract proofs and runtime ideas into step-by-step visuals made the material more intuitive and easier to explain.

Why I Built This

In class, many ideas are presented in pseudocode or theorem form. I wanted a tool that showed the algorithm state evolving in real time, so I could directly connect correctness arguments with what the algorithm is doing at each step.

The site follows a common step-engine pattern: algorithm logic emits events and the UI renders those events with controls for stepping, playback speed, and backtracking.
